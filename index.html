Define:

  • Login Block-For: Defend against DoS attacks by disabling logins after a specified number of failed login attempts
  • Access Control List: Passwords are the first line of defense, stolen or weak one can provide the attacker easy access to corporate data
  • Quarantine: Tracking down and identifying infected machines within the contained areas
  • Inoculation: All uninfected systems are patched with the appropriate vendor patch
